Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective: April 2026

Yommo is built for collectors, by collectors. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work. We do not sell your data, we do not serve you ads, and we never will. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what rights you have over your information.

1. Who We Are

Yommo is a manga, comic, and light novel collection tracking application. References to "Yommo", "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to the Yommo app and its developer. You can contact us at support@Yommo.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to provide the service.

  • Account information: Your email address, used to create and identify your account.
  • Display name: An optional name you choose when signing up, shown in your profile.
  • Collection data: Books, series, volumes, prices, reading status, ratings, and notes that you enter manually or via scanning.
  • Wishlist data: Books you add to your wishlist including titles, priority, and notes.
  • Public profile data: If you choose to make your profile public — your username, bio, and collection. This is strictly opt-in and off by default.
  • Referral data: If you use or share a referral code, we record the association to apply the reward.
  • Crash and error reports: Anonymous crash reports sent via Sentry when the app encounters an error. These include the error type, device OS version, and app version. They do not include your collection data or personal information.
  • Anonymous usage data: Basic events such as "item added" or "scan used" logged without any personally identifiable information, used to improve the app.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not access your contacts or address book.
  • We do not read your camera roll or photo library beyond the image you actively choose to scan.
  • We do not collect payment information. All purchases are processed by Apple or Google directly through their in-app purchase systems.
  • We do not serve advertising and have no advertising partners.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party.
  • We do not build advertising profiles or sell behavioral data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account and sync your collection across your devices.
  • To power the app's features — scanning, search, stats, wishlist, referrals, and public profiles.
  • To process your Pro subscription status via RevenueCat.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs using anonymized error reports.
  • To send push notifications you have explicitly requested (such as release day reminders). You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure, hosted on AWS in the United States. Supabase uses row-level security to ensure your data is only accessible to you (or, for public profiles, only the data you have chosen to make public).

We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Your password is never stored in plain text — authentication is handled by Supabase Auth using industry-standard hashing.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete all associated data — your collection, wishlist, profile, and referral history — within 48 hours of your request.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you. Use the Export (CSV or JSON) feature in Settings for your collection data.
  • Correction: Update or correct your information directly in the app at any time.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data. See Section 9 below.
  • Portability: Export your full collection as CSV or JSON from Settings at any time.
  • Opt out of communications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, these rights are provided under GDPR. For California residents, these rights are provided under CCPA. To exercise any right, email us at support@Yommo.app.

9. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by:

  • Tapping Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app, or
  • Emailing support@Yommo.app with the subject line "Delete My Account".

We process all deletion requests within 48 hours. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

10. Children's Privacy

Yommo is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@Yommo.app and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you via a notice in the app or by email before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
